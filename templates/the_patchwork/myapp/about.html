Our Story

How The PatchWork™ Began: A Journey of Helping People and Businesses Grow

In 2017, Patch Wetzel started The PatchWork™ with a big dream: to help businesses become the best they can be and to support people who need a hand. Our story is about more than just business—it’s about making a real difference in the world. We started with a simple goal: to help everyone, everywhere, reach their highest potential.

From our first project to our global reach today, we’ve been working hard to bring smiles and success to people and businesses all around the world. Join us on this exciting journey and see how we’re making a positive impact everywhere we go!
